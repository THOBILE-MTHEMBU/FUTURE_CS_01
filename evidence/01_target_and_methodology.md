# Target and Methodology

## Selected Target

- Website: `testphp.vulnweb.com`
- Reason selected: public training target suitable for ethical, read-only review

## Why the Target Was Appropriate

The website publicly presents itself as a testing and learning target. This made it appropriate for a non-destructive internship exercise focused on observation, reporting, and remediation planning.

## Assessment Constraints

- Passive techniques only
- No exploitation
- No brute force
- No service disruption

## Evidence Collection Approach

Evidence in this repository was gathered from:

- public website content
- passive scan summaries
- public header and port exposure views
- publicly accessible diagnostic output

## Reporting Standard

Each finding was written to answer four business questions:

1. What is the issue?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What is the risk level?
4. What should be fixed first?
